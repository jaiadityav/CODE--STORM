The Problem

Every return costs sellers money

  • 📦
    Reverse Shipping
    ₹80–250 per returned item in courier fees alone, paid by the seller.
  • 🏭
    Restocking & Inspection
    Returned items must be inspected, repackaged, or discarded — adding hidden labour costs.
  • 💸
    Refund Processing
    Platform fees are rarely refunded. Sellers absorb payment gateway + marketplace commission.
  • 📊
    Listing Suppression
    High return rates lower your seller score, reducing visibility in search results.
